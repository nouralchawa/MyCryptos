WELCOME TO MY CRYPTO

MY CRYPTO is an application which allows you to perform virtual investments in cryptocurrencies and see the evolution of your operations.

Your global position can be checked at the Status page. Here you will be able to see the "Amount invested" and the "Profit of investment" in euros if you were to withdraw all your criptocurrencies at the actual market price.
